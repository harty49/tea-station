about

top tea

At Top-Tea, we source from the top 2% of all the teas produced in the world. Our mission is to create a delicious experience and in order to do so, we believe in providing out communiTEA with the highest quality product we can. All teas, herbs and botanicals that come into Top-Tea are quality tested in our lab at our warehouse in LA, California. All products received and shippedd out of Top-Tea go through stringent GMP and HACCP business practices and handling. Additionally, we do all that we can to ensure the highest level of sanitation and employee safety.


We do all that we can to ensure that a tea that you order in January looks, smells, and tastes the exact same as when you order it in June. We do all we can to ensure the freshest, best-tasting tea for you. We are so honored to have you as a part of our community. We believe that we are here to impact as many lives as we can through tea so thank you for being a part of that journey!
